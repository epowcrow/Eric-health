Getting the Most Out of This App
Tips for accurate data and meaningful trends
❤️ Blood Pressure
🌅Best time: First thing in the morning, before Losartan, before coffee or food. Most clinically meaningful reading.
🪑Before you take it: Sit quietly 5 minutes. Feet flat, back supported, arm at heart level. Don't talk during reading.
2️⃣Take two readings: Wait 1 minute between. Log both — the app averages your daily readings automatically.
🌆Evening reading: Around 8-9pm, 1+ hour after eating or exercise. Helps identify time-of-day patterns.
🚫Avoid logging right after: Exercise, eating, caffeine, stress, or just waking up. Wait 5-10 minutes.
📈Trends matter most: One high reading isn't alarming. Your weekly average and direction is what counts.
🥗 Food Logging
🔍Search first: The database has 600+ foods and brands. Fastest and most accurate for common items.
📷Scan barcodes: For any packaged food — pulls exact label data. Tap Scan in the Food tab.
✦Use AI for: Restaurant meals, custom dishes, combinations. Describe naturally — "chipotle burrito bowl with chicken and guac."
⚖️Adjust portions: Every food entry has a portion stepper. Use it — "1.5 servings" makes a real macro difference.
⏱Log as you eat: Don't wait until end of day — memory fades and portions get guessed poorly.
🧂Don't forget condiments: Soy sauce, dressings, sauces — sodium hides here. Log them separately.
💧 Water · ⚖️ Weight · 🚴 Workouts
💧Water: First glass when you wake up. Tap + every time you finish one throughout the day.
⚖️Weight: Same time every morning, after bathroom, before eating. Ignore daily swings — watch weekly trends.
🚴Workouts: Log immediately after — takes 10 seconds. Include all active time including warmup.
💊 Medication
⏰Set a daily alarm: Same time every day. Missing doses lets BP creep back up fast — as you saw tonight.
🌅Sequence matters: Log BP first, THEN take Losartan. Your pre-med reading is your true baseline.
📊 Reading Your Numbers
✅Optimal BP: Below 120/80 — your goal.
🟡Elevated: 120-129 systolic. Lifestyle changes, watch closely.
🟠High Stage 1: 130-139 / 80-89. Medication likely needed.
🔴High Stage 2: 140+ / 90+. See your doctor. Don't miss meds.
🧂Sodium goal: Under 1,500mg/day. Most Americans eat 3,500mg+.
💪Protein goal: 180g/day at your size to preserve muscle while losing weight.
